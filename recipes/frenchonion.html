French Onion Soup

Description

The trick to making good french onion soup is to deeply caramelize the onions. Degalze with red wine, and use a good quality Gruyere cheese. I personally like to use a combination of Vidalia and Spanish Yellow onions to add some sweetness. I use soy sauce instead of salt to add some extra umami, and use a good quality beef stock. In my experience, deglazing with Sherry works the best.

Ingredients

  • 5 Onions
  • Butter and Olive Oil
  • Thyme
  • Black Pepper
  • Sherry
  • Soy Sauce
  • Beef Stock
  • Gruyere Cheese
  • French Bread

Steps

  1. Caremalize the onions until deep golden brown in the butter, olive oil, and thyme
  2. Turn up heat and deglaze with Sherry
  3. Add beef stock, and continue cooking for 45 minutes
  4. Add soy sauce and black pepper to taste
  5. Ladle soup into crocks, add french bread and cheese to crocks and bake at 400 until cheese slightly browned
